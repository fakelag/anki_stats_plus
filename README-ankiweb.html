Stats Plus allows you to include the Anki graphs inside of other screens. You can choose the ones you want, and order them as you like! Configure the graphs under "Tools > Add-ons > Stats Plus > Config". You can add graphs to three different screen:
  • Deck Browser: This is the screen that Anki opens with, and where you see your deck hierarchy (screenshot top left)
  • Deck Overview: When you click on a deck to study it, you first see the Deck Overview. This is where you see the deck description, and have a "Study Now" button (screenshot top right)
  • Congrats Page: This is the screen, where Anki congratulates you, after you finished all cards in a deck (screenshot bottom right)
